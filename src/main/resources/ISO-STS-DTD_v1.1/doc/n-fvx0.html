<part-title>

Part Title in a Citation

Title of part of a book (for example, chapter, module) described in a bibliographic reference.

Any combination of:

  • Text, numbers, or special characters
  • <email> Email Address

  • <uri> Uniform Resource Indicator (URI)
  • <bold> Bold
  • <italic> Italic

This element may be contained in:

<element-citation>, <mixed-citation>, <related-article>, <related-object>
