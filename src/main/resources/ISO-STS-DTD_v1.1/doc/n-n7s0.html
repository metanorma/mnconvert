International Organization for Standardization (ISO)

<sub-part>

Sub-part of Standard or Standards-Related Document

A numbered sub-part appearing physically within a part, but with its body and back matter, typically presented with its own table of contents and pagination.

Remarks

This element is part of the ISOSTS customization of the JATS journal publishing vocabulary; it is not part of the base vocabulary.

Attribute

part-type Type of Part

Model Description

The following, in order:

  • <label> Label of a Figure, Reference, Etc., zero or one
  • <title> Title
  • <body> Body of the Document
  • <back> Back Matter, zero or one

This element may be contained in:

<body>
